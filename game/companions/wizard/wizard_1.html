"Ah, this should work.", he says pointing at a strange diagram in the book.

He points at you chanting a strange language, "Iachau, tynnwch glwyfau'r rhai a anafwyd."

As he says this a warm sensation fills your body and you feel your wounds start to lessen.

Now, let's get to the city.
