You walk towards the warrior, staggering due to your head injury.
The warrior looks at you, noticing the blood pouring out of the wound on your head,
which seems to be getting worse by the minute. The smile drops from his face, 
he looks at you with a expression of great concern.
 
"Are ye alricht?" He says with a hearty tone. "Whit happen tae ye?"

You explain to him, that you woke up the in woods with no memory of what happened to you.

"That's terrible", he says. "We need tae get ye tae a healer o' some sort. 
Theres one in the nearby city, A wis headin thare anyway, 
ye micht as well join me for the journey."
You agree to go with him to the nearby city, to find a healer.
