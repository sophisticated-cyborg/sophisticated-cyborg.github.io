You've walked for what seems like forever.
The pain in your head seems to have returned and is getting worse than before.

"Thare's the gates now tae the city thare.", he says pointing at this huge stone
gateway with what seems to be a vast city behind it.
You quickly approach the gates, as the guards step out in front of you. 

"Who goes there?", they say readying their weapons.

"Khurroud Blackforge, A'm takin ma friend here tae see the healer, 
it seems he wis attackit i the woods no ower long ago."

"Alright, you may pass." Says the guard.
As you walk through the gate, things start to get blurry.
You pass out...
